Profile
Hey there! I'm Rudy, and I enjoy applying technology to neuroscience.

What this means is that I build software simulations and models of neural networks. This is useful because such simulations can aide in scientific research. For example, if you want to know how long memory lasts then modeling can help you answer this question.

Some examples of what I have done can be found in my portfolio.

Although this software has been my primary focus for about a year now, I have also been writing scripts, websites, and other closely-related works for general use and fun for over three years.

R and Javascript are my go-to programming languages, but I often incorporate other languages and tools within my work.


Education
I completed my Bachelor's degree in Biology at Saint Louis University, where I concentrated in cell biology and neuroscience. After finishing, I have continued to pursue my work and have been coding and modeling neuronal systems ever since.

My programming skills are almost entirely self-taught where they were developed from a hobbyist level to a career tool.
